MPAS-Atmosphere Meshes

Several resolutions of quasi-uniform meshes, plus one refined mesh, are available for download. Each download provides an SCVT mesh on the unit sphere, the mesh connectivity (graph.info) file for the mesh, and partitionings of the mesh (e.g., graph.info.part.32) for various MPI task counts. Other meshes may be available upon request from the MPAS-Atmosphere developers by sending mail to mpas-atmosphere-developers AT googlegroups.com.

Variable-resolution meshes

All variable-resolution meshes are supplied with a single refinement region that is centered on 0.0 degrees latitude, 0.0 degrees longitude. This region of refinement may be relocated to other locations on the sphere using the grid_rotate utility, whose usage is described in the MPAS-Atmosphere Users' Guide.
