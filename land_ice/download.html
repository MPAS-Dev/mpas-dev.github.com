MPAS-Albany Land Ice Public Releases

As of May 2021, MPAS-Albany-Landice is developed within the E3SM repository. MALI development occurs in a development repository at https://github.com/MALI-Dev/E3SM. (Development in that repository is periodically merged into the primary E3SM repository, https://github.com/E3SM-Project/E3SM.) The MALI code is located in the subdirectory components/mpas-albany-landice, and can be compiled as a stand-alone land ice model, or within the E3SM coupled climate model.

Download and build instructions for the Albany multiphysics package that includes the the First-Order velocity solver in MPAS-Albany Land Ice can be found at the Albany repository.
